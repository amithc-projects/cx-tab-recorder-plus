⚡ Enabling the Keyboard Shortcut

To open the popup instantly using Alt+Shift+Q, you may need to enable it manually in Chrome:

  1. Copy and paste this into your browser address bar: chrome://extensions/shortcuts
  2. Find OmniCapt in the list.
  3. Click the Pencil Icon ✏️ next to "Open Recorder Popup".
  4. Press your desired keys (e.g., Alt+Shift+Q).

That's it! Now you can open the recorder without clicking the mouse.

1. How to Record

  1. Press Alt+Shift+Q or click the toolbar icon.
  2. Press R (or click Start Recording).
  3. To stop, press Ctrl+Shift+S.

2. Annotation & Screenshots

  • Annotate: Press Alt+Shift+A to open the drawing toolbar.
  • Screenshot: Press Ctrl+Shift+E to save a screenshot to your computer.
  • Clipboard: Click the "Copy" icon on the toolbar to copy the screenshot to your clipboard.
